Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

Stoic Wisdoms ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to provide you with the best experience:

  • Email & Name (Optional) — If you choose to provide them during onboarding for our newsletter and updates
  • App Usage Data — Anonymous analytics to understand how features are used and improve the app
  • Subscription Status — Managed securely through Apple's StoreKit (we never see your payment details)

Information Stored Locally

The following data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to our servers:

  • Your reflection scores and streak data
  • Favorite quotes
  • App preferences and settings
  • Reminder schedules

How We Use Your Information

  • To send you weekly wisdom emails and app updates (only if you opted in)
  • To improve app features based on anonymous usage patterns
  • To provide customer support when you contact us

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Apple StoreKit — For processing subscriptions (Apple handles all payment data)
  • Supabase — For securely storing email addresses (if provided)

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Ever.

Data Retention

If you provide your email, we retain it until you unsubscribe or request deletion. Local app data is retained until you delete the app.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Unsubscribe from emails at any time
  • Delete all local data by removing the app

Children's Privacy

Stoic Wisdoms is suitable for all ages (rated 4+). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes through the app or via email.

We believe in minimal data collection. We only collect what's necessary to provide you with a great experience.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the 30-day free trial work?

When you subscribe, you get full access to all features for 30 days completely free. You won't be charged until the trial ends. You can cancel anytime during the trial in your iPhone's Settings → Subscriptions.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to iPhone Settings → tap your name at the top → Subscriptions → Stoic Wisdoms → Cancel Subscription. You'll keep access until the end of your current billing period.

Can I get a refund?

Refunds are handled by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com, sign in with your Apple ID, and select the purchase you'd like to request a refund for.

How do I restore my purchase on a new device?

Open Stoic Wisdoms on your new device, go to the subscription screen, and tap "Restore Purchases". Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used for the original purchase.

How many quotes are included?

Stoic Wisdoms includes 249 authentic quotes from 13 Stoic philosophers including Marcus Aurelius, Seneca, Epictetus, and more. All quotes are sourced from original historical texts.

How does the Daily Reflection work?

Each day you'll receive a quote with reflection questions. Swipe through the cards to engage with the wisdom. Your responses contribute to your streak and help track your growth across 8 Stoic principles.

Do the widgets update automatically?

Yes! The widgets update daily at midnight with a new quote. You can add widgets by long-pressing your home screen → tap "+" → search for "Stoic Wisdoms".

Is my data backed up?

Your streak and reflection data is stored locally on your device. If you have iCloud backup enabled for your iPhone, this data will be included in your backup.

Can I use the app offline?

Yes! All 249 quotes are stored in the app. You can read quotes, do reflections, and use most features without an internet connection.

How do I unsubscribe from emails?

Click the "Unsubscribe" link at the bottom of any email we send, or contact us directly and we'll remove you immediately.
